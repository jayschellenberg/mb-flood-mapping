Manitoba Flood Zone Screening

Checks a subject property against published provincial flood extents. For appraisal context only.

Disclaimer. This tool is based on flood mapping published by the Manitoba Government Data MB geoportal (1997/2009/2011 Red River flood extents; Manitoba 1-in-200 Year Flood Extent), the Manitoba Land Initiative (Red River Valley and Lower Red River Designated Flood Areas, under the Water Resources Administration Act s.17), and Natural Resources Canada (Canada Flood Map Inventory — a study-coverage index, not flood extents). Polygons are simplified for display; geocoding is by Mapbox with an OpenStreetMap Nominatim fallback. Provided without warranty of any kind, express or implied. Not a legal survey, engineering flood study, insurance determination, or site-specific flood protection level assessment. Verify any determination against the authoritative source. Per NRCan, the absence of an area from the Canada Flood Map Inventory does not mean no mapping exists.

Enter a civic address (e.g. "123 Main St, Winnipeg, MB") OR paste coordinates (lat, lon). From Google Maps: right-click a location, then click the coordinates at the top of the menu to copy them to clipboard.

Distances via Turf.js (spherical). Polygons simplified (~50 m) for responsiveness. Geocoding by OpenStreetMap Nominatim.
